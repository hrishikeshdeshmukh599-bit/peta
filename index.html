Make a Donation to PETA India

Together, we can make the world a better place for all beings. Your donation will go to work instantly to help animals who are suffering and dying in vivisection laboratories, on factory farms, in zoos and circuses and in the leather and fur industries.

PETA India (People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals) is India's leading animal rights organization, working since 2000 to end animal abuse in labs, food, clothing, and entertainment, advocating for veganism and animal welfare through investigations, campaigns, public education, celebrity involvement, and policy work, with millions of supporters promoting compassion for all species. They focus on areas where animals suffer most, like circuses, the leather trade, and food industries, promoting plant-based living and ethical treatment.


Key Focus Areas:

  1. Laboratories: Campaigns against animal testing.
  2. Food Industry: Promotes veganism with campaigns like "I am ME, Not Mutton".
  3. Clothing Trade: Advocates against leather and fur, promoting vegan fashion.
  4. Entertainment: Works to ban animals in circuses and other abusive forms of entertainment.
  5. Companion Animals: Addresses breeding and abandonment issues.
